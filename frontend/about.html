About

starttmux

A free browser game that teaches tmux through puzzles and a playful simulator — built for people who live in coding agents but have not yet made friends with the terminal.

What you get

Learn — ten guided puzzles across a golden path and a level-up track. Cheat sheet — shortcuts and file basics. Simulator — split panes, spawn fake agents, run pretend tests and deploys. Everything runs in your browser; nothing touches your machine.
